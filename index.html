Background

Christian Writers Association is a community of Christian authors, book editors, book designers, publishers, book reviewers, bloggers, and book marketers based in Uganda.

Mission

Writers rising in inspiration, awareness and ability, creating a climate for a far better world: Jesus' kingdom.

Objectives

  1. Provide a safe place of companionship and support
  2. Provide a learning environment
  3. Provide broad information on the world of writing and
  4. Help each other access and take up opportunity
  5. Help nurture and develop young talent

Benefits

  1. Build power and visibility that comes from being a community of like-minded people.
  2. Develop understanding and professionalism from others.
  3. Discover friends to whom one can open up, sure that they are safe and their ideas wont be plagiarized.
  4. Develop writing discipline alongside others and build quality output.
  5. Find motivation for writing and living.
  6. Find a place to share one's insights, knowledge and experience
